Being a Teaching Assistant

The Keller Hall Atrium, which is where I held office hours twice a week, for students.

In January 2023, I accepted an offer to be an undergraduate Teaching Assistant for CSCI 1933: Intro to Data Structures & Algorithms. This position involved and helped me develop a lot of time management, coordination, leadership, and communication skills. My main duties were to lead labs and hold weekly office hours so that I can help students in the class get a better understanding of Java and other intermediate programming concepts. I also helped in the administrative side of things with grading projects using JUnit tests and grading midterms with a provided rubric, as well as editing/updating lab and project writeups using LaTeX.
