Volunteer Youth Commissioner:

Image source

I was a Volunteer Youth Commissioner for the Burnsville Parks and Natural Resources Commission, for two terms, from July 2019-June 2021. I essentially gave feedback, from a high schooler's perspective, on what can be done to improve the city's parks, lakes, trails, and other natural spaces.

The 2021 Youth Award:

I was a recipient of the 2021 Youth Award from the Minnesota Commission of the Deaf, Deaf-Blind, and Hard of Hearing.
